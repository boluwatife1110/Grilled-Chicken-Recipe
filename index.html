Grilled Chicken Recipe

git

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 small flour tortillas

Instructions:

  • Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  • Season chicken with cumin, salt, and pepper.
  • Grill chicken for 5-6 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  • Grill bell peppers and onions for 3-4 minutes per side, or until tender.
  • Warm tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  • Assemble fajitas with grilled chicken, bell peppers, and onions.
